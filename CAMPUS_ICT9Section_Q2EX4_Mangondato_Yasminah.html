No Easy Album

Noeasy is the second studio album by South Korean boy band Stray Kids. It was released on August 23, 2021, through JYP Entertainment, nine months after its predecessor, Japanese extended play All In and eleven months after the Korean reissue In Life. The physical album comes in three versions: two standard editions (A and B) and a limited edition. It comes with the photobook, lyric book and the 2 photocards.
